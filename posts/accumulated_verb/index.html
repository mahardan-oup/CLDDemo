accumulated

verb əˈkjuːmjəleɪtɪd
accumulated is the past participle of accumulate

  1. to accumulate things is to collect them or pile them up
  2. things accumulate when they form a heap or pile
  3. to increase in quantity

Etymology: from Latin cumulus ‘heap’

Root word: accumulate (verb)

Related roots: None

Related words: accumulates (verb), accumulating (verb), accumulation (noun)
