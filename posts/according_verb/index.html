according

verb əˈkɔːdɪŋ
according is the present continuous tense of accord

  1. to be consistent with something
  2. to give or grant someone a power or privilege

Root word: accord (verb)

Related roots: accord (noun)

Related words: accords (verb), accorded (verb)
