account

noun əˈkaʊnt

  1. a description or story about something that happened
  2. an amount of money someone has in a bank or building society
  3. a statement showing how much money someone owes, a bill
  4. an arrangement to use a computing or social media service
  5. a statement of the money a person or organization owes or has received
  6. If you have a bank account, you have money in a bank.
