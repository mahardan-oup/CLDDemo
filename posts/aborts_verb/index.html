aborts

verb əˈbɔːts
aborts is the third person present form of abort

  1. to abort a plan or mission is to cancel it after it has begun
  2. to put an end to something before it has been completed

Etymology: from Latin aboriri ‘to miscarry’

Root word: abort (verb)

Related roots: None

Related words: aborting (verb), aborted (verb)
