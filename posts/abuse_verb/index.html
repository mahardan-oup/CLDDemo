abuse

verb a-bewz əˈbjuːz OR əˈbjuːs

  1. to abuse something is to treat it badly and harm it
  2. to abuse someone is to say unpleasant things about them
  3. to abuse someone also means to hurt them or treat them cruelly
  4. to use something badly or wrongly; to misuse something
  5. to say unpleasant things about a person or thing
