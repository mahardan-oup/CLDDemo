accountants

noun
accountants is the plural form of accountant

  1. a person whose job is to write and organise the money accounts of a person or organisation
  2. someone whose job is to record and organize the money a person or organization spends and receives

Root word: accountant (noun)

Related roots: None

Related words: accountancy (noun)
