accurate

adjective ˈækjərət

  1. correct, done exactly and carefully
  2. correct or exact; giving the right information
  3. exactly correct or exactly in the right place

Etymology: from Latin cura ‘care’, also the source of our words curious and secure

Root word: accurate (adjective) is a root form in the accurate word family.

Related roots: None

Related words: accurately (adverb), accuracy (noun)
