abandoned

verb əˈbændənd
abandoned is the past participle of abandon

  1. to abandon something or someone is to go away and leave them, without intending to go back
  2. to stop doing something when it becomes impossible

Root word: abandon (verb)

Related roots: abandon (noun)

Related words: abandons (verb), abandoning (verb), abandonment (noun)
