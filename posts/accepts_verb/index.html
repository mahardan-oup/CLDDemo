accepts

verb əkˈsepts
accepts is the third person present form of accept

  1. to accept something is to take it when someone offers it to you
  2. to accept an invitation is to say ‘yes’ to it
  3. to accept an idea or suggestion is to agree that it is true or worth thinking about
  4. to take a thing that is offered or presented to you
  5. to say yes to an invitation or offer
  6. to take or say yes to something that someone offers you
  7. To accept a fact or situation means to agree or realise that it exists.

Root word: accept (verb)

Related roots: None

Related words: accepting (verb), accepted (verb), acceptance (noun)
