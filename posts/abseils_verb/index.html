abseils

verb ˈæbseɪlz
abseils is the third person present form of abseil

  1. to abseil is to lower yourself down a steep cliff or rock by sliding down a rope

Etymology: from German, from ab ‘down’ and Seil ‘rope’

Root word: abseil (verb)

Related roots: None

Related words: abseiling (verb), abseiled (verb)
