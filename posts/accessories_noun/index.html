accessories

noun əkˈsesəriz
accessories is the plural form of accessory

  1. an extra or spare part that goes with something bigger
  2. an item like a piece of jewellery or a handbag that goes with clothes
  3. a thing which can be added to something else to make it more useful or attractive
  4. a person who helps someone else to commit a crime, without actually taking part in it
