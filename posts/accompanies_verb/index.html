accompanies

verb əˈkʌmpəniz
accompanies is the third person present form of accompany

  1. to accompany someone is to go somewhere with them
  2. to accompany a singer is to play the piano or another instrument while they sing
  3. to go with somebody to a place
  4. to happen or belong together with something else
  5. to play music, usually on a piano, to support a singer or another musician

Root word: accompany (verb)

Related roots: None

Related words: accompanying (verb), accompanied (verb)
