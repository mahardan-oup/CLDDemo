acclaim

verb əˈkleɪm

  1. to acclaim someone is to welcome or praise them warmly
  2. to praise someone or something publicly or officially

Etymology: from Latin clamare ‘to shout’

Root word: acclaim (verb) is a root form in the acclaim word family.

Related roots: acclaim (noun)

Related words: acclaims (verb), acclaiming (verb), acclaimed (verb), acclamation (noun)
