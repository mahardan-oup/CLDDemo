acids

noun
acids is the plural form of acid

  1. a substance that contains hydrogen and causes chemical change. Acids are the opposite of alkalis
  2. a chemical substance that contains hydrogen and neutralizes alkalis. The hydrogen can be replaced by a metal to form a salt.
