abode

verb əˈbəʊd
abode is an inflection of abide

  1. you cannot abide something or someone when you don’t like them at all
  2. to abide by a rule or promise is to keep to it

Root word: abide (verb)

Related roots: None

Related words: abides (verb), abiding (verb), abided (verb)
