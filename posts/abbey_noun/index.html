abbey

noun ˈæbi

  1. a group of buildings where monks or nuns live and work
  2. a church which is or was part of an abbey, such as Westminster Abbey in London
  3. a monastery or convent
  4. a church that was once part of a monastery, for example Westminster Abbey in London
