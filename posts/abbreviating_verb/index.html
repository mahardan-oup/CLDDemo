abbreviating

verb əˈbriːvieɪtɪŋ
abbreviating is the present continuous tense of abbreviate

  1. to abbreviate a word or phrase is to write it with fewer letters so that it is shorter

Etymology: from Latin brevis ‘short’, also the origin of our word brief

Root word: abbreviate (verb)

Related roots: None

Related words: abbreviates (verb), abbreviated (verb)
