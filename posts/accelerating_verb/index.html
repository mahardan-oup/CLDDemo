accelerating

verb əkˈseləreɪtɪŋ
accelerating is the present continuous tense of accelerate

  1. to accelerate is to go faster
  2. to start to go or drive faster
  3. to make something happen more quickly

Etymology: from Latin celer ‘swift’

Root word: accelerate (verb)

Related roots: None

Related words: accelerates (verb), accelerated (verb), acceleration (noun)
