academies

noun əˈkædəmiz
academies is the plural form of academy

  1. a college or school
  2. a society of scholars or scientists who meet to discuss their work

Etymology: fromAkademeia, the name of a garden near Athens, where the Greek philosopher Plato taught his pupils. It was named after the legendary hero Academus.

Root word: academy (noun)

Related roots: None
