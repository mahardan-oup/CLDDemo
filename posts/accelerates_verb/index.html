accelerates

verb əkˈseləreɪts
accelerates is the third person present form of accelerate

  1. to accelerate is to go faster
  2. to start to go or drive faster
  3. to make something happen more quickly

Etymology: from Latin celer ‘swift’

Root word: accelerate (verb)

Related roots: None

Related words: accelerating (verb), accelerated (verb), acceleration (noun)
