accident

noun ˈæksɪdənt

  1. something unexpected that happens, especially when something is broken, or someone is hurt or killed
  2. an unexpected happening, especially one causing injury or damage

Etymology: from Latin accidere ‘to happen’

Root word: accident (noun) is a root form in the accident word family.

Related roots: None
