accomplishing

verb əˈkʌmplɪʃɪŋ
accomplishing is the present continuous tense of accomplish

  1. to accomplish something is to do it successfully
  2. to do something completely and successfully

Etymology: from Latin complere ‘to complete’

Root word: accomplish (verb)

Related roots: None

Related words: accomplishes (verb), accomplished (verb), accomplishment (noun)
