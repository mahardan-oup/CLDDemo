abdomen

noun ˈæbdəmən

  1. the part of your body below your chest, which contains your stomach and intestines
  2. the back part of the body of an insect or spider
  3. the lower front part of a person’s or animal’s body, containing the stomach, intestines, and other organs used for digesting food
  4. the rear section of an insect’s body
