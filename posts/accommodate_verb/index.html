accommodate

verb əˈkɒmədeɪt

  1. to accommodate someone or something is to provide them with a place to stay
  2. to accommodate someone also means to do what you can to please them
  3. to provide somebody with a place to live, work, or sleep overnight
  4. to help someone by providing them with what they need
  5. to provide a place big enough for someone to sit, sleep, etc.

Root word: accommodate (verb) is a root form in the accommodate word family.

Related roots: None

Related words: accommodates (verb), accommodating (verb), accommodated (verb)
