accusing

verb əˈkjuːzɪŋ
accusing is the present continuous tense of accuse

  1. to accuse someone is to say that they did something wrong

Etymology: from Latin causa ‘cause’

Root word: accuse (verb)

Related roots: None

Related words: accuses (verb), accused (verb), accuser (noun), accusation (noun)
